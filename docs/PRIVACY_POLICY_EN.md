# Privacy Policy — RPG Tracker

_Last updated: 2026-07-07_

**RPG Tracker** (the "App") is a gamified tracker for tasks, habits and goals. We
respect your privacy. This document explains what data is processed and how.

## What data is processed

- **Your content.** Tasks, habits, goals, steps, completions, earned XP/currency,
  purchased customization, and settings. By default this is stored **only on your
  device** (a local SQLite database).
- **Technical data.** The App does not collect analytics and does not use
  third-party advertising SDKs.

## Storage and transfer

- **Local (default).** Without an account, data never leaves your device.
- **Cloud sync (optional, once accounts are available).** If you sign in, your
  content is synced to a secure backend (Supabase) so you can use multiple
  devices. Data is scoped to your user id and protected by row-level security;
  other users cannot access it. Signing in is fully optional — the App works
  offline without an account.

## Permissions

- **Notifications.** Used for task and habit reminders at times you choose.
  Requested on demand and revocable in system settings.
- **Exact alarms / full-screen notifications (Android).** To deliver reminders at
  the scheduled time, including under battery-saving modes.
- **Planned sensitive permissions (future versions, strictly optional and off by
  default):** usage access, Accessibility service, and display-over-other-apps —
  only for "hard focus"/distraction-blocking features. Each is enabled manually
  with a dedicated explanation and consent; the App is fully functional without
  them.

## Children

The App is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect their
personal data.

## Data deletion

Local data is removed when you uninstall the App. When using a cloud account, you
will be able to request deletion of your account data.

## Changes

We may update this policy. The current version is always available in the App's
repository and/or store listing.
